Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I check service availability in my area?

You can check service availability by calling our sales team at (888) 552-6525 or using our online availability checker on our website. Simply enter your address, and we'll let you know what services are available in your area.

What do I need for installation?

Our professional technicians will bring all necessary equipment. You just need to be present during the scheduled installation window and ensure access to the areas where equipment will be installed.

How can I pay my bill?

You can pay your bill online through our customer portal, by phone at (888) 552-6526, by mail, or set up automatic payments from your bank account or credit card.

What if I'm experiencing technical issues?

Contact our 24/7 technical support team at (888) 552-6526. Our experts can often resolve issues remotely, or we'll schedule a technician visit if needed.
