Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and plans

Are there any setup fees?

No, we provide free professional installation for all residential plans. Business plans may have a one-time setup fee depending on the complexity of the installation.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect on your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract required?

We offer both contract and no-contract options. Contract plans may offer better rates, while no-contract plans provide more flexibility.

What's included in the pricing?

All plans include the listed internet speeds, TV channels, basic equipment rental, professional installation, and 24/7 customer support.
